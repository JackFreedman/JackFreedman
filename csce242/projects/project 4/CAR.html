Halo

The halo is a titanium shaped wishbone that goes around the drivers head to protect them during flipping in accidents and from any debris flying at them like tires. Research was done into the halo and it showed that they are capable of deflecting a wheel fired at it at 150mph

Survival Cell

It is made to be near indestructible so during crashes the driver will be safe inside of it. It is built from 6mm of exceedingly strong carbon fiber composite with a layer of Kevlar

Fuel cell safety advancements

The Fuel Tanks are made from Kevlar and are designed to be highly durable. They are made like this so in the event of a crash fuel does not spill out of the car and potentially cause a fire.
