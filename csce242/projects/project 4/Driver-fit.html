Formula One Helmets

The helmet is fitting with Impact Sensors that measure the level of impact the driver's head sustains during a crash

Biometric Gloves

They are built with sensors that are able to monitor the driver's vital signs, such as pulse and oxygen levels which can be vital in making sure a driver is ok on the track and nothing is going wrong during the race.

Fire retardant suit

The suits are tested to make sure they can withstand being heated to 600 to 800 degrees for more than 11 seconds
