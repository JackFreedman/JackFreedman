Weiner Dogs

By Jack Freedman

Dauchsund dogs

    Dauchsund dogs, or better known as weiner dogs, are one of the coolest dog breeds. People call them weiner dogs because they have a similar shape as a hot dog. They are very small dogs which make them easy to care for compared to bigger dogs like golden retrievers.
